Achievments

Inventure is ranked #1 in Karnataka and Bangalore, and #3 in India in the Education World C Fore Survey 2020 (Co Ed Day Schools Category). Inventure has ranked amongst the Top 10 schools in India for the last three years.

Inventurers have got admissions in leading colleges across India and the rest of the world, including Harvard, Stanford, University of California Berkeley, University of Pennsylvania, Brown University, Carnegie Mellon University, Cambridge University, Harvey Mudd, Georgia Tech, National Law University, JIPMER Pondicherry and Ashoka University.

Inventure has featured in Fortune India Magazine’s list of ‘Future 50 Schools Shaping Education in India’. Nooraine Fazal,Co-founder, CEO & Managing Trustee of Inventure, is among Fortune Magazine’s ‘Future 50 Leaders Shaping Success’

Inventure received ‘Most Innovative Practice’ award by Tata Communications and Parikrma Humanity Foundation for its Changemaker Program in 2018 and ‘Most Innovative Idea’ award for its Empathy Program in 2019.(The Changemaker program teaches students how to be advocates of positive change in their communities. The Empathy Program is aimed at helping students better understand themselves and their emotions, be more empathetic towards others, nature and their environments, as well as their communities.)

Inventure is a part of Round Square, a select group of 200 schools worldwide which facilitates student and faculty exchange opportunities & learning / sharing of best practices globally.
